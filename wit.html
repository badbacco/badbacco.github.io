What Is Tobacco?

Tobacco is a green leafy plant often grown in warmer climates. Tobacco contains nicotine which is a unusually addictive substance often used in bug killers. Tobacco can come in forms like cigarettes, pipe,and vaporized cigarettes where you smoke and inhale the tobacco. That is harmfull mostly for your lungs. More forms of tobacco are smokeless tobacco which you snuff and chew, and their are also hookah pipes. Hookah pipes is a type of water pipe then the tobacco burns in a bowl then the smoke from the tobacco goes into the pipes.
